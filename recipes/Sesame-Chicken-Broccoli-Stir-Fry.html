Sesame Chicken & Broccoli Stir-Fry

3 Points® per serving

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp(s) Soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp(s) Ketchup
  • 4 tsp(s) Dark brown sugar
  • 2 tsp(s)Sambal oelek
  • 2 tsp(s) Cornstarch
  • 2 tsp(s) Sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp(s) Minced garlic
  • 12 oz Frozen chopped broccoli
  • 1 cup(s) Reduced-sodium chicken broth
  • 8 oz, diced Cooked skinless boneless chicken breast(s)
  • ½ cup(s), sliced ½ cup(s), sliced
  1. In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, ketchup, brown sugar, sambal oelek, and cornstarch; reserve.
  2. Heat sesame oil in a large non-stick skillet or wok over medium heat. Add garlic and ginger and cook for 1 minute. Add broccoli and toss to coat. Pour chicken broth over broccoli, raise heat, cover and cook for 5 minutes or until broccoli is crisp-tender. Stir in chicken and reserved sauce and simmer for 3 minutes or until thickened. Garnish with scallions.
  3. Serving size: 1 cup
½ cup(s), sliced
